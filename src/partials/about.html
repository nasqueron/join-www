What we stand for

At Nasqueron, we believe in the power of open-source software and the limitless possibilities it brings to the world of technology. As an open-source software community, we are dedicated to creating an inclusive and collaborative environment that fosters innovation and growth.

Founded in 2014, Nasqueron has become a vibrant community of passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, united by a shared love for software development and free culture. Our community members hail from all corners of the globe, from Africa, Europe and North America, bringing unique perspectives, skills, and ideas to the table.

We focus on free culture, ethics and to be a positive change. Our software is open source while our datasources and content are freely licensed. We share values like respect, justice and equity.

How we roll

Collaboration lies at the heart of Nasqueron. We believe that great things happen when talented beings come together to work towards a common goal. Through our collaborative efforts, we want to develop and maintain a wide range of projects, tools, and libraries that make a significant impact in the software development landscape, especially in automation, software quality and infrastructure.

Openness is a fundamental principle we uphold. We are fully committed to open-source ideals, which means that our software is freely available, modifiable, and accessible to all. By promoting transparency and encouraging contributions from the community at large, we foster an environment where creativity can flourish and software can evolve organically.

Quality is of paramount importance to us. We strive for excellence in every aspect of our work, from the code we write to the documentation we provide. By prioritizing quality, we ensure that our software is robust, reliable, and user-friendly, delivering value to those who use it.

Our culture

But it's not all work and no play at Nasqueron. We believe that software development should be enjoyable and fulfilling. We embrace a culture of fun, where creativity is celebrated, and beings are encouraged to bring their unique personalities and interests to their work. We understand that when people enjoy what they do, they produce their best work.

If you are passionate about open source software, eager to learn, and ready to collaborate with like-minded individuals, we'll be happy to welcome you, as an internship at Nasqueron is an opportunity to grow and contribute to exciting projects.

Join our community, and together, we can shape the future of software development while creating a friendly and welcoming space for all.
