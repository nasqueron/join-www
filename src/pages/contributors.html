Open-source contributions

The purpose of this site is to showcase the various opportunities available at Nasqueron.

While we provide internships and a formal mentoring program to achieve our outreach and community growth objectives, we're primarily a free culture and open-source project. As such, we welcome contributions, and you're free to participate freely, without joining a specific program.

What's Nasqueron?

🏴 Nasqueron is a budding community of creative people, writers, developers and thinkers.

🌱👨🏽‍🤝‍👨🏼🧑🏾‍🤝‍🧑🏻🙋🏾‍♂️👩🏾 You'll find here like-minded people to connect to, hang out, and build projects.

❤️ We like experiments, originality and to discover new things.

👽 We are Nasqueron.

Development servers

We maintain development servers where every tool to build and contribute is installed.

You can have access to it to contribute to Nasqueron and other open-source or free culture projects, host personal pages, have a FreeBSD shell. We also have a separate shell service for your IRC connection, and a Docker server to build images or test new services.
