Internship

What can you do at Nasqueron?

You can contribute to various roles, with opportunities to discover several aspects of how we work.

Interested? We invite you to get in touch and provide us with the necessary information to apply for the internship opportunity.

Development

We mainly write software in Rust, Python, C, PHP and Node.JS

Focus is on automation, infrastructure, data analysis, communication and systems programming.

SRE & Operations

Gain hands-on experience with DevOps and SRE practices, automation tools, and infrastructure management.

Contribute to building and maintaining robust, scalable, and reliable systems. Our stack includes FreeBSD, Linux, Docker, Salt, PostgreSQL, MariaDB, ElasticSearch and in-house tools.

Security

Focus on security-related projects, including vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, or enhancing the security posture of our software and infrastructure.

We work with an open infrastructure and want to be transparent and open, that creates specific challenges as "security" by obfuscation is clearly not an option on the table.

Design & UX

Dive into the world of design and UX, an open-source design and prototyping.

Contribute to creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces for our software projects.

Be our next intern and join our community

We are thrilled to offer you the opportunity to contribute to Nasqueron in various roles and explore the diverse aspects of our work.

We encourage you to get in touch with us and provide the necessary information to apply for the internship opportunity that aligns with your interests. We look forward to working with you, sharing knowledge, and creating impactful contributions together.
